# <span style="color:#fabd2f">Animal cells
## Cytoskeleton
- Unique `internal structure` of animal cells
- Gives animal cells their `shape`
- Helps `cell organelles move` around the cell
### Centrosomes
- Part of the cytoskeleton
- USed during cell divison

## <span style="color:#fabd2f">Flagella, cillia
- Flagella --> Long, whip-like
- Cillia --> short, hair-like

## Cytoplasm
- Similar to that of plant cells (no difference)

### Smooth endoplasmic reticulum
