# <span style="color:#fabd2f"> Circulatory System
## <span style="color:#fabd2f">Blood
- Connective tissue
- Liquid extracellular matrix --> `Plasma`:
  - Almost 50% of blood
  - Composition:
    - 90% --> Water
    - 10% --> Proteins, nutrients, hormones, ions
- Types of cells:
  - Red Blood Cells
  - White Blood Cells
  - Platelets

### <span style="color:#fabd2f">Red Blood Cells
- No nucleus
- Donut shape
- 5*10<sup>6</sup>/mm<sup>3</sup> concentration
- Produced by: Red bone marrow
- Lifespan: 120 days
