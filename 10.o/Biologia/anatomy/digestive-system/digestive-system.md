# <span style="color:#fabd2f">Digestive System I.

- 3 parts:
  - Foregut (mouth-->stomach)
  - Midgut ()
  - Hindgut
- `Food:`
  - nutrients --> Proteins, fats, carbohydrates
  - minerals
  - vitamins --> Help enzymes function
  - water
  - roughage
    - Provides `nutrition for symbiotic bacteria`
    - Helps food keep moving through the GI tract



## <span style="color:#fabd2f">Steps of nutrition
- Ingestion --> `Physical breakdown` of food
- Digestion --> `Chemical breakdown `of food (via `enzymes & hydrolysis`)
- Absorption --> Nutrient monomers enter the `circulatory/lymphatic system`
- Assimilation --> `Macromolecules are synthesized` from nutrients
- Egestion --> Undigested `waste is removed`

## <span style="color:#fabd2f">Oral cavity
- Contains:
  - Teeth
  - Tongue
  - Salivary glands

- Saliva:
  - Can break down starch
  - Forms the bolus
  - Allows for taste

- Tongue:
  - Important for swallowing/ingestion
  - Mixes food with saliva
  - Used for speaking



## Digestive juices
- Saliva:
  - Amylase
  - Mucus
  - Lysozyme
- Gastric juice
  - HCl + Pepsin
- Bile
  - Emulsifier
- Pancreatic juice
  - contains multiple enzymes for digestion
- Intestinal juice
