# <span style="color:#fabd2f"> Midgut

## <span style="color:#fabd2f">Small intestine
- `Longest part` of the GI tract
- Forms coils for space efficiency
- Folds, Villi & Microvilli
  - Large surface area
  - Villi contain blood & lymphatic vessels
- Two glands:   
  - Liver
  - Pancreas

## <span style="color:#fabd2f">Role of the liver
- Conversion of `nutrients into glucose`
- Synthesis of `glycogen from glucose`
- `Detoxification`
### <span style="color:#fabd2f">Production
  - Bile --> `Emulsifier` for fat digestion
  - Proteins for blood clotting
### <span style="color:#fabd2f">Storage
  - Iron
  - Lipids
  - Vitamins (DEKA)

## <span style="color:#fabd2f">Pancreas
### Two major parts
- `Endocrine` part
  - Produces `insulin`
- `Exocrine` part
  - Produces `pancreatic juice`

## <span style="color:#fabd2f"> Large intestine
- No digestion, only absorption (of minerals & water)
- Symbiotic bacteria `digest cellulose` and `produce vitamins`

### <span style="color:#fabd2f">Dimensions
- Length: 1.5m
- Diameter: 6-7cm
