## <span style="color:#fabd2f">Eating disorders
### <span style="color:#fabd2f">Orthorexia
- Proposed disorder
- Excessive preoccupation with healthy food
    <h4>Symptoms</h4>
- Severe restriction or elimination of food groups
- Avoidance of restaurants
##
### <span style="color:#fabd2f"> "Bigorexia"/adonis syndrome
- AKA muscular dysmorphia
- Essentially reverse anorexia
### <span style="color:#fabd2f"> Anorexia
<h4> Symptoms</h4>
- Abnormally low body weight
- Intense fear of gaining weight
- Distorted perception of weight
