# <span style="color:#fabd2f">Immune system
- Basis of immune response: Recognizing own cells from foreign cells
- Antigen: Foreign particle that triggers immune response


## Types of immunity
## Active
- `The body` is still producing antibodies
- Happens when the body is infected
### Natural Active
- Natural infection
### Artificial Active
- Happens with `vaccination with weakened pathogen`
## Passive
- The antibodies are being produced by another organisma
### Natural
- Getting antibodies through placenta/breastmilk
### Artificial
- Vaccination with antibodies


|Type|RBC|Plasma|Transfusion recv|Transfusion give|
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
|A|A-type antigen|Can have antibodies against B|From B/O|To A/AB|
|B|B-type antigen|Can have antibodies against A|From A/O|To B/AB|
|AB|Both types of antigen|No antibodies|From any|To AB|
|O|No antigens|Can have antibodies against Both|From O|To Any|
