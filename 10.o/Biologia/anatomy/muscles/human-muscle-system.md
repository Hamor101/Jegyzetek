# Human muscle system

## Shapes of skeletal muscle organs
### Pennate / spindle-shpaed
- Parallel
- Only slightly wider in the middle

### Convergent
- Converging from a broad origin to a single point


### Sphincters
- Round
- Pupillary sphincter --> contols how much light is let into the retina
- precapillary sphincters --> control how much blood is allowed into capillaries

## Muscles of the head
## Mimic muscles
- Attached only to the skin of the face
- Contribute to feeding & speaking
## Sternocleidomastoid
- Used for rotating the head
