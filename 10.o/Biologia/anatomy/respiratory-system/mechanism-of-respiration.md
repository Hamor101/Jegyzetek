# <span style="color:#fabd2f">Mechanism of breathing
- Lungs have `no skeletal muscle` --> Breathing is done by changing the volume of the chest cavity
- Main muscles used for breathing:
  - Diaphragm
  - Intercostal muscles

## <span style="color:#fabd2f">Vital capacity
- `Total lung capacity`: ~5l
- During `normal breathing`, only `~0.5L gas is exchanged` --> Vital volume

## <span style="color:#fabd2f"> Breathing control
- Stimuli for breathing:
  - CO<sub>2</sub> Content of blood
  - O<sub>2</sub> Content of blood
- Chemoreceptors detect increase in CO<sub>2</sub> content + decrease in O<sub>2</sub> content --> Triggers inhalation
- Mechanoreceptors detect strain/stretch in lungs --> Triggers exhalation
