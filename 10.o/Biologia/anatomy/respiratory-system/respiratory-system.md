# <span style="color:#fabd2f">Respiratory system
## <span style="color:#fabd2f"> Main functions
- Transport air into lungs
- Blood gas exchange 
- Smelling
- Speech
- Air filtration
- Heating & Moistening Air
- Excretion (gets rid of excess water)
