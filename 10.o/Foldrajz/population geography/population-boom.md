# <span style="color:#fabd2f">Population boom
- Up to the start of the 19th century, population was around `1 billion`
- World population started growing, showing an exponential trend
- Greatest increase: `20th century`
- Uncertain if growth will slow down
#
- Population growth started mainly in Europe & America
- Highest annual growth rate: 1962/1963

## <span style="color:#fabd2f">Reasons for pop. growth
- More developed production
- Industrialization
  - More efficient `food production` (machinery, chemicals, etc.)
- Better `housing conditions` (indoor toilet, electricity, clean tap water, etc.)
- Better `healthcare` (new medications, vaccines, antibiotics)
  - Lower infant mortality
