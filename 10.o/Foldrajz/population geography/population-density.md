# <span style="color:#fabd2f">Changes in population

## Formula:
  - $\Delta PS = (CBR+I)-(CDR+E)$
  - where:
    - CBR --> Crude Birth Rate (births per annum)
    - CDR --> Crude Death Rate (deaths per annum)
    - I --> Immigration
    - E --> Emigration
### Crude Birth Rate
$CBR = \frac{Newborns}{Total\space Population}*1000 = \underbrace{}‰$
- Hungary: ~9‰
- Afghanistan: ~36‰
### Crude Death Rate
$CDR = \frac{Deaths}{Total\space Population}*1000$
- Developed countries: 3-15‰
- Third-world countries: 15-20‰
### Natural growth
$NG = CBR - CDR$
