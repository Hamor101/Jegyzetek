# <span style="color:#fabd2f"> Settlement geography
- Settlement: A `separate place` on earth, where a `group of people of any size` `live together`
- Types:
  - Mobile settlements
  - Permanent settlements


## <span style="color:#fabd2f">Mobile settlements
- approx. `30-50mln` people live in them
- Usally have a `nomadic herding` lifestyle (keeping horses, bactrian camels)
- No fixed houses (Yurts / Tents)
- E.g:
  - Mongolians
  - Saami people (from Lapland)
  - Maasai people (East africa)
