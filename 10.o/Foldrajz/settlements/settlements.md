# <span style="color:#fabd2f"> Settlement geography
- Settlement: A `separate place` on earth, where a `group of people of any size` `live together`
- Types:
  - Mobile settlements
  - Permanent settlements


## <span style="color:#fabd2f">Mobile settlements
- approx. `30-50mln` people live in them
- Usally have a `nomadic herding` lifestyle (keeping horses, bactrian camels)
- No fixed houses (Yurts / Tents)
- E.g:
  - Mongolians
  - Saami people (from Lapland)
  - Maasai people (East africa)
  - Some random aah ppl in the US (sun belt)

# <span style="color:#fabd2f">Permanent settlements
- Three types:
  - Villages
  - Farms/Ranches/Hamlets
  - Cities/Towns

## <span style="color:#fabd2f"> Villages
- Most ancient type of settlement (~8-15k years ago)
### <span style="color:#fabd2f">Origin:
  - Initital purpose for appearance: Agricultural production
  - After the last ice age, `plant cultivation` began
  - Plant cultivation `requires sedentary lifestyle` --> First permanent settlements appeared in the `middle east/asia minor`
### <span style="color:#fabd2f">Groups by purpose
  1. `Agriculture`
  2. `Fishing`
  3. `Mining` --> E.g Úrkút
  4. `Tourism` --> E.g Velence
