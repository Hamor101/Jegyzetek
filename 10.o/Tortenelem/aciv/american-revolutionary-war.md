#  <span style="color:#fabd2f">American war of Independence

## <span style="color:#fabd2f">Causes
- 7 Years war (France <--> England) --> England needed more money
- Excessive `taxation without parliamentary representation`
- Boston massacre
- East India Company had a `monopoly on tea trade`
- `Sales tax` on tea --> `Boston tea party` as a means of `protest`
- Taxes by British parliament:
  - Sugar act -- 1764
  - Stamp act -- 1765
  - Townshend acts -- 1767 & 1768
  - Tea act -- 1773
- England `sent soldiers to Boston` in response
