# <span style="color:#fabd2f"> Eastern europe

## <span style="color:#fabd2f">Agriculture
- Growth of population --> `greater demand` for food
- Greater demand for food --> `higher crop prices`
- Increasing price of `crops` --> Increase in `profit`
- This caused a `lack of industrial development` (no manufactories, guilds remained dominant)
- Landlords seized the chance to export agricultural goods
- To increase agricultural output, `peasants were made to work more for free` (robot / socage / corvee)
- the payment of the ninth in crops was reintroduced
