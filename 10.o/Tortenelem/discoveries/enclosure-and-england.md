# <span style="color:#fabd2f"> Changes in england around the time of the discoveries

 
## <span style="color:#fabd2f">Wool
- New commodity
- Easier to produce & more valuable than crops
- Fields were converted into grazing lands
- Created the 'gentry' --> new kind of business-minded nobility
#
## <span style="color:#fabd2f"> Enclosure
- After black death --> Less workers
- Less workers --> More bargaining rights for peasants
- Common lands became private, `peasants were expelled`
- Newly private lands were `fenced off`
- Expelled peasants often moved to cities to work in [manufactories](effects-of-the-discoveries.md/#manufactories)
