# <span style="color:#fabd2f">English revolution (1640-89)

## <span style="color:#fabd2f">Stuarts (1603-1649)
- James I & Charles I
- Stuarts ruled without a parliament

### The short parliament
- War with `Scotland` --> Financial trouble
- `1640` --> Charles I `summons parliament`
- Charles I wanted the parliament to `vote in new taxes`
- Parliament had conditions:
  - Parliament had to be called at a `regular interval` --> (The ruler couldn't dissolve parliament)
  - Parliament had to `approve new taxes`
  - No `special courts`
  - The parliament wanted to `control the army`
- Charles didn't like these conditions
- Charles tried to dissolve `the House of Commons` --> Civil war
