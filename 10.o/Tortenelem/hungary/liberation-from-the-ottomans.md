# <span style="color:#fabd2f">Liberation of Hungary from Ottoman rule
- After the tripartition, cultural contact remained between the three parts
- First person to call for reunification --> Zrínyi Miklós


## <span style="color:#fabd2f">Siege of Vienna
- The commander of the Ottoman forces was made to kill himself

## <span style="color:#fabd2f">Reasons for the liberation of Hungary
- The turkish army:
  - Didn't modernize
  - Was exhausted by the end of the campaign against Vienna
- The habsburgs had a regular army

## <span style="color:#fabd2f">After the liberation
- Hungarian nobles `promised to always elect Habsburg descendants` of the current emperor
- `Nobles gave up their right to oppose the king` (as defined in the `Resistance Clause` of the Golden Bull)
- Habsburgs occupied Hungary --> Made nobles `buy their land back` (with a 90% discount)
- `Taxes were raised for Serfs`
  - Serfs had to `accomodate & transport` Habsburg soldiers
- End of the liberation:`1699`
