# <span style="color:#fabd2f">The reformation in Hungary


## Arrival & spread of Lutheran ideas
- `1520s` --> First arrival of Lutheran ideas
- Lutheran ideas first spread by german priests and students from Wittenberg
- Popularity:
  - `German speakers` living in `Upper Hungary`
  - Royal family (the `king's wife`)
- By the end of the 16th century, protestantism was the most widespread form of Christianity in Hungary
- 1549
- `Nobles` mainly became `Calvinists`
- Protestant preachers roamed the country debating and preaching (in Hungarian)
- Preachers inspired `trust & hope within the peasants`



## <span style="color:#fabd2f">Helping factors
- Concentration on the `struggle for the throne` --> Neither ruler wanted to `persecute protestants`
- The Ottomans `didn't care as long as they paid tax`
