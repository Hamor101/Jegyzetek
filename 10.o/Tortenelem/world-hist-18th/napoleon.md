# <span style="color:#fabd2f">Napoleon Bonaparte
- 1799 --> Rose ot power through a coup d'etat
## <span style="color:#fabd2f">Domestic policy
- Restored order:
  - Eliminated robber bands
- Created a Police state:
  - Had a network of informers and spies
  - Introduced state censorship of the press
  - New constitution did not grant civil rights
- Centralized the executive & administrative system:
  - Districts were governed by prefects appointed by him
  - Prefects appointed mayors
  - Self-government and election of officials was abolished
- Contributed to cultural development
  - Regulated secondary and high schools
- Tried and amanaged to get widespread social support
  - Made a Concordat with the Pope
  - Peasants could keep the land they had gained during the revolution
- Preserved some achiements of the revolution
  - Created the Code Napoleon with two basic principles: Equality before law & sanctity of property
- He was aware of the importance of the economy
  - Established the bank of france
  - Had a mercantilist economic policy
